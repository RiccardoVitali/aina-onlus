Distance adoption

AINA adheres to the “Guidelines for Distance Support of Children and Young People” issued by the Agency for Onlus. It operates in compliance with the principles indicated in the Guidelines, guaranteeing supporters and beneficiaries transparency and quality in projects, clear and complete information on the contribution paid in support of children and their communities. AINA has its own page in the List of SaD Organizations established by the Agency for Onlus Organizations at the following link. Distance Support is a form of solidarity that allows many, young and old, to receive education, food, clothes, education, family help, without having to leave their country of origin.
